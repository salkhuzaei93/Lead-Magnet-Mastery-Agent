# Competitor Lead Magnet Audit Template

## Instructions
Complete one table per competitor. Aim for 8–12 competitors minimum.
Opt into each lead magnet where possible — the delivery experience matters as much as the promise.
Date each audit — competitive intelligence expires in 90 days.
